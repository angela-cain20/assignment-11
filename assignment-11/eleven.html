Artist analysis of my favorite artist....

Manic
by Halsey




Halsey's Manic is an emotion-filled, genre-blending studio album released in 2020. The album pays homage to her rise to fame, as well as her journey to find her place in the world, both personally and artistically. Songs explore themes of mental health, ambition, heartbreak, and self-discovery. The tracklist is an eclectic mix of electronic, hip hop, and rock, with Halsey's stunning vocals guiding listeners on a journey of pure emotion. Manic is an honest, intimate and unique look into Halsey's life and a must-listen for fans of her groundbreaking music.

— She also makes poetry, sold at Barnes and Noble.





Halsey's poem book, titled "I Would Leave Me If I Could," is a powerful collection of poems expressing her deepest thoughts and emotions. Her words paint a vivid picture of love, loss, and all of the complexities of being human. From exploring the depths of her soul to understanding the beauty of pain, her poetry is filled with rawness and truth.
